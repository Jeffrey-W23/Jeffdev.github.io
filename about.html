About Me

Background:

Hello, my name is Thomas. I'm a hard-working, passionate games programmer that is currently living in Melbourne Australia. Over the last 2 years, I have been studying at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE) to obtain a Bachelor of Games and Virtual Worlds (Programming).

My passion for programming started when I joined a fan project attempting to recreate the video game Journey to the centre of Hawkthorne, shown in episode 3.20 of NBC's Community. I started with creating sprite-sheets for many different characters which this soon lead me to explore the various scripts to figure out how all this stuff was coming together. The game was being built with the Love2D engine (https://love2d.org) and while I didn't do any code as a part of my contributions to this game it did push me in the direction of making my own games with this engine.

One of the first game creations I ever completed in Love2D was a game I called Zombie Game. This game ignited a fire in me that didn't stop for 3 straight months of development, only slowing down for a job that I started at my local Mc Donalds. I took a break from programming entirely during this time to try and get some money saved for potential university or moving out.

Like most, I got very sick of spending all my time making burgers so I applied for a job at a local Software Development Company called Advance Computing. They were offering a position for a software development traineeship which was perfect for someone like me that was eager to get a start in the software development industry. I gained many important skills at Advance, made many great friends and got my start in programming. This traineeship eventually pushed me towards my university degree at the Academy Of Interactive Entertainment. I have learnt more than I could ever imagine at AIE and I can't praise this academy enough for the inspiration it has given me for games and programming.

Interests:

While I'm not at university or work I love to play games, Overwatch specifically at the moment but I love any type of game that I can play and communicate with other people (eg. PUBG, Rocket League, Dead by Daylight).

Other activities I enjoy are Podcasts, Movies, TV Shows and Music, I watch/listen to a very wide variety of content because almost everything catches my interest (Over a hundred shows and counting).

Collecting is another interest of mine I love to do when I have the money, I love to collect old video game stuff (Specifically old Nintendo stuff) and vinyl records.
