Gypsy's Tale

Gypsy's Tale is a small third person adventure game, where players take control

of Gypsy, an inquisitive, cheeky Golden Retriever. Run around and cause mischief in an

inner-city park interacting with families, steal frisbees, finding bones and making friends.

Toy Wars: Un-Bear-Able Warefare

Toy Wars pits two hot-seated players against each other in a turn-based PvP combat environment.

Both players have their own set of Toy Soldiers and must use them to destroy

either their opponents Soldiers, or their Teddy Bear.

Zombie Game

Zombie Game is a retro-style top-down zombie survival shooter. It's the story of a nameless

survivor in an empty, desolate world with nothing but the clothes on his back

and a loaded pistol to make it through the day.

AIE Assignment: Artificial Intelligence

This was a project that I completed in my first-year studies at AIE to show my understanding

and knowledge of Artificial Intelligence. The project is written in C++ and includes

many code examples other than AI, for example, LinkedLists and Associative Arrays.
